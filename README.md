# Prax - Your Solo AI Forge

Prax comes from **Praxis**: turning theory into practice.

Prax is an open-source product for people who want to become a super-individual by building real AI projects, not just consuming tutorials.

## Why Prax

Most AI learning content teaches concepts.  
Prax focuses on one thing: **finish a practical project in 30 minutes, ship it, and turn it into your personal capability asset**.

It combines three loops in one system:

1. **Learn** - project-based AI learning path
2. **Build** - convert finished projects into portfolio-ready artifacts
3. **Share** - publish reusable workflows for others to fork

## Who It's For

- People in transition (job-seeking, career switch, rebuilding direction)
- Solo founders and one-person companies
- Non-technical or semi-technical knowledge workers with AI anxiety
- PMs and creators who want to prove capability with shipped outcomes

## Product Vision

Prax helps users move from:

- "I saved 100 AI tutorials"  
to
- "I built 10 reusable AI workflows and can prove my ability publicly"

## Core Value Proposition

Prax is both:

- A **learning engine** (how to build)
- A **personal brand engine** (how to show what you built)

## MVP Scope (Phase 1)

Focus only on the Learn layer first.

### What MVP includes

- 3-5 project-based learning modules
- Each module can produce one working output in ~30 minutes
- Standard module structure: `Context -> Goal -> Steps -> Run -> Extend`
- Starter templates for common solo workflows
- Bilingual documentation (Chinese first, English-friendly structure)

### Candidate first modules

1. AI weekly digest generator
2. Multi-platform content repurposer
3. Competitor tracking + summary bot
4. Local knowledge Q&A assistant (Retrieval-Augmented Generation, RAG)
5. Personal workflow orchestration starter

## Design Principles

- **Action over theory**: every module must end with a runnable output
- **Low friction**: copy-paste runnable steps for non-technical users
- **Portfolio-first**: output should be displayable as personal proof of work
- **Open by default**: reusable, forkable, transparent
- **Coach, don't overwhelm**: reduce anxiety and increase execution confidence

## Success Metrics (North Star)

- First project completion rate within 30 minutes
- Number of published reusable workflows per user
- Number of forks per workflow
- Percentage of users generating portfolio assets
