# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in Prax a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and constructive
- Giving actionable feedback
- Focusing on ideas, not personal attacks
- Welcoming different experience levels and backgrounds

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, hate speech, or discrimination
- Personal insults or trolling
- Publishing private information without permission
- Repeated bad-faith disruption in issues or PRs

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards and may remove comments, commits, issues, or contributions that violate this code.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and external communication representing the project.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, open a private issue or contact maintainers through repository channels.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take any action deemed appropriate, including warning, temporary suspension, or permanent ban from project participation.
